Cape May

April 16 - 17, 2012

Lauren and I ended up taking a weekend trip to Cape May, staying at a little bed and breakfast. Despite being April, the weather was pleasant giving us a great time to sit out on the beach and relax. Wandering around town we were able to take in some beautiful houses. We ended up spending the evening at Sunset Beach and the Cape May Lighthouse.
