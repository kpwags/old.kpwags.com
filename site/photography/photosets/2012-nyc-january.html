New York City

January 13 - 15, 2012

Thanks to a deal, Lauren and I took our first visit to New York City in 2012. It was the first time we went where we didn't stay in Manhattan, instead opting for Long Island City in Queens. Fortunately we were right by a subway stop and were able to make it into Manhattan rather easily to walk around and explore. The other benefit is we ended up being within walking distance of some piers on the East River giving us a great view of the New York City skyline.
