DSLR Experimentation

January 22, 2012

I've always liked the pictures I've seen where people take long exposures. Given that it was nicely peaceful after a snowfall, I decided I'd take my new Canon 60D and a tripod outside and see what I could do.

I really liked how the lights on the buildings looked.

I made this for my wife and she seemed to enjoy it.
