New York City

August 10 - 11, 2012

Our third and final trip to New York City was a hot one. It was hot and humid the entire weekend, but we were able to see the Space Shuttle Enterprise at the Intrepid Air and Space Museum as well as Grand Central Station, Top of the Rock and MoMA.

I was finally able to see a Space Shuttle up close. Seeing Enterprise was unreal to me
